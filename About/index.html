About us

Sthaapna is trying to ignite young minds and give platform to their innovative ideas and initiatives through community building and a model of entrepreneurship. Following the approach of nationalism and welfare, we are striving to create a society which has active participation of the youth. We aim to spread awareness about our glorious past, Indian culture and philosophy, which is the essence of this soil. Thus, through a wide range of contests, Sthaapna is trying to engage the youth and make them aware about Indian warriors, eminent leaders and unsung heroes, and enhance in them self-esteem about their own nation. Sthaapna is tirelessly working towards empowering the Indian youth, who, in the future, can help in the nation-building process. We aim to channelize the youth energy in the right direction and make them aware of the glorious Indian history which is unknown even today





Our Vision

For the longest time, India has been a market of cheap labor. We have provided the world with the best of minds and ideas at a penny price. The reason why the worldâ€™s largest democracy, whose maximum population is youth, is a mere market for labour is that we lack opportunities within the national boundaries. Sthaapna dreams of changing this scenario and making India the most prominent recruiter of great minds. It moves forward with the mission of making India self-sufficient to cater to the need of opportunities in the growing youth.



Our Mission

The organization is giving a platform to young minds to imagine, ignite, and invent. Standing on the principles of nationalism and welfare, the organization has a vision of bringing the country together with logic, reasoning, and true nationalism. We aim to channelize the youth energy in the right direction and make them aware of the glorious Indian history which is unknown even today.
